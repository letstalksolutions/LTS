Building Smarter, Not Just Faster

I'm Andreas Nyberg, a Technical Architect and Atlassian SME with over 15 years of experience delivering enterprise ITSM solutions. I specialise in Jira Service Management, Atlassian Assets (CMDB), workflow automation, and integrations across Azure, GCP, and Google Workspace.

My approach is hands-on, pragmatic, and focused on outcomes. I don't just implement tools—I design systems that scale with your business and empower your teams.
