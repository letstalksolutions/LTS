Kantar Media: ITSM Transition at Scale

Platform Migration CMDB Design Integration

The Challenge

Kantar Media needed to migrate from ServiceNow to Jira Service Management for 4,500+ users across HR, IT, SOC, NOC, and service agent teams. The transition required a complete CMDB rebuild, integration with Azure DevOps, and minimal disruption to operations.

The Solution

  • Designed Atlassian Assets (CMDB) structure covering location hierarchies, people, workstations, network gear, and server infrastructure
  • Built MVP for 500 initial users, ahead of schedule for full 2026 rollout
  • Integrated JSM with Azure DevOps to enable seamless handoffs between service requests and development workflows
  • Delivered knowledge transfer sessions enabling internal teams to independently configure automation rules and CMDB queries

Outcomes

  • 500 users onboarded ahead of schedule
  • Complete CMDB structure operational and integrated with automation
  • Internal teams trained and self-sufficient for ongoing configuration

Datasparq: ISO 27001 Documentation Hub

Knowledge Management Compliance Automation

The Challenge

Datasparq needed to consolidate scattered ISO 27001 policies and process documentation into a single, auditor-friendly knowledge base. The company also required cost optimisation for GCP and Atlassian spend.

The Solution

  • Designed and delivered proprietary ISO 27001 Confluence Documentation Hub, praised by auditors for clarity and structure
  • Migrated internal support from Zendesk to JSM, streamlining ticket workflows and aligning support with engineering teams
  • Built cost optimisation automations for GCP and Atlassian using webhooks and APIs, reducing SaaS overhead
  • Created Confluence knowledge bases for clients and staff, improving self-service adoption

Outcomes

  • ISO 27001 audit completed with zero documentation gaps
  • Support ticket resolution time improved by 25%
  • Measurable reduction in SaaS costs through automation

Zephr (Zuora): Global Support Operations

Service Desk Workflow Design Team Scaling

The Challenge

Zephr needed to scale technical support for tier-1 clients (WHICH?, New York Post, News Corp Australia) across multiple time zones, while preventing customer-facing incidents through better escalation workflows.

The Solution

  • Built and scaled B2B technical support teams in UK & New Zealand
  • Designed escalation workflows integrating Jira, PagerDuty, and Slack to prevent incidents
  • Led company-wide Jira administration, optimising workflows and ticket management
  • Established 24/5 global support model, improving customer coverage and satisfaction

Outcomes

  • 24/5 global support coverage operational within 6 months
  • Escalation incidents reduced by 40%
  • Customer satisfaction scores improved across all tier-1 accounts
