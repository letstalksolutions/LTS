Services Designed to Scale

Whether you're starting fresh or optimising an existing Atlassian environment, I provide tailored consulting to help your team work smarter, not harder.

Platform Consultation

Expert guidance on Atlassian product selection, implementation, configuration, and optimisation for your specific business needs.

What's Included

  • Product selection aligned with your goals and budget
  • Implementation planning and execution
  • Custom configuration tailored to your workflows
  • Performance tuning and ongoing optimisation

Who It's For

Teams evaluating Atlassian tools for the first time, or organisations looking to consolidate scattered systems into a unified platform.

Workflow Optimisation

Streamline your team's processes with custom workflows, automation rules, and integrations that boost productivity and reduce manual overhead.

What's Included

  • Workflow analysis and bottleneck identification
  • Custom workflow design for Jira and JSM
  • Automation rules to eliminate repetitive tasks
  • Ongoing refinement and continuous improvement support

Who It's For

Teams struggling with inefficient processes, slow ticket resolution, or manual handoffs that slow down delivery.

Systems Integration

Connect your Atlassian tools with other business systems—CRM, DevOps, cloud platforms—for seamless data flow and enhanced collaboration.

What's Included

  • Integration planning and architecture design
  • API development (REST, webhooks)
  • Azure DevOps, GCP, Google Workspace, and custom integrations
  • Testing, documentation, and handoff to your teams

Who It's For

Organisations with fragmented toolsets, relying on manual data entry or struggling to maintain a single source of truth.
